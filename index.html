About MKOUR

MKOUR (Make Your Own) isn’t just another sneaker label — it’s a youth-driven movement born out of SRMC’s entrepreneurial spirit. Built by students, for students, we believe India deserves sneakers that are authentic, aspirational, and affordable.

Why We Exist

While global giants price out young Indians and local brands imitate without originality, MKOUR breaks the cycle with design-first, student-led creativity.

Design Ethos

Inspired by Mercury — speed and style. Minimal, trend-forward designs for Gen Z and millennials.

Fair Pricing

Every pair priced between ₹1,200 and ₹1,800 — premium feel without the premium tag.

The Team

16–17 passionate student creators. Early stage, experimenting with outsourced manufacturing and preparing for market launch.

Brand Philosophy

More Than Shoes — A Culture of Self-Expression.
